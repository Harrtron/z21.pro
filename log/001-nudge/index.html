project 001 — nudge

a couples app. not a reminder app.

built to reduce friction between two people who care about each other — but are busy.

this is the first product shipped under z21. from zero to live on the app store.

the problem

most relationship tension isn't dramatic.

it's repetitive.

  • small requests left unacknowledged
  • missed appreciation
  • things left unsaid

not because people don't care. because attention is finite.

over time, that compounds.

nudge is built around a simple idea: make small acts visible. make appreciation easy. reduce the friction loop.

how it works

one partner adds something they need help with. the other completes it. it's marked done in a satisfying way. appreciation is sent.

that's the core loop.

no guilt metrics. no points scoring.

just action → recognition → momentum.

around that loop we've added:

  • check-ins — structured emotional signals without heavy conversations
  • love notes — prompted appreciation that lands at the right time
  • treats — lightweight rewards that feel intentional
  • moments — small prompts to interrupt drift

each feature exists to reinforce the same outcome: stay aligned. stay connected. without turning it into work.

who it's for

not couples in crisis.

couples in motion.

two people juggling jobs, life, responsibilities — who don't want connection to fade into background noise.

download for ios android — coming soon

scope

three core screens. one primary loop. intentionally simple.

this is v1.
